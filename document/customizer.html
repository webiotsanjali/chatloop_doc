Menu

This group settings are responcible for managing menus. All menus location is available for filling with any of the predefined menus.



Menus also available for the "Appearance → Menus" option.

Widgets

Widgets are bloks of content that can be placed into specifically designed widget areas.
You can also find list of available widgets under Appearance → Widgets.



Widget
Depends on the selected page that is available for editing with Customizer's tools, you can notice a different kind of widgets included to a particular page. The same can be done through Appearance > Widgets .



New Widgets
  1. click on "Add a Widget" button to see the available widgets, then choose the one you want to use



    2. Reorder the position of widgets
    1. Move - moves the widget to another sidebar.
    2. Down - moves the widget down.
    3. Up - moves the widget upwards.

Home Page Settings

Important! These settings are also available for editing through Settings → Reading section.
